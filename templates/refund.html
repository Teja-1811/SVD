Refund Policy

Refund & Cancellation Terms

This policy applies to confirmed delivery orders, recurring supply orders and invoice payments accepted by Sri Vijaya Durga Milk Agency.

Refund Eligibility

  • Missed or undelivered orders
  • Quality issues reported immediately after delivery
  • Incorrect product supplied

Non-Refundable Cases

  • Products already consumed
  • Complaints raised long after delivery
  • Wrong address or customer unavailability at delivery time

Refund Method & Timeline

Refunds may be processed through bill adjustment or the original payment method depending on the case. Eligible refund adjustments are normally handled within 5 to 7 working days after review and approval.

Cancellations

Customers should inform cancellations in advance where possible. Same-day delivery requests that have already been packed, dispatched or delivered may not be eligible for cancellation.
