Order Flow

How Orders And Payments Work

This page explains how Sri Vijaya Durga Milk Agency handles order requests, delivery confirmation, invoice generation and approved payment collection.

1. Product Review

Customers review products and pricing on the website or contact us directly.

2. Order Request

Existing customers may log in to the portal and place an order request.

3. Confirmation & Delivery

Our team confirms quantity, address, schedule and payable amount before fulfilment.

4. Invoice & Payment

Invoices and dues are shown to the customer for settlement through approved modes.

Orders We Support

  • Daily household milk delivery
  • Monthly subscription delivery
  • Retail and wholesale supply to shops
  • Bulk orders for hotels and businesses

Online Payment Usage

Online payment is used only for approved dues and confirmed invoices.

  • Bill payment by existing customers
  • Settlement of monthly invoices
  • Bulk order billing after confirmation
