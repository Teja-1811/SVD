About Sri Vijaya Durga Milk Agency

Delivering Fresh & Quality Milk — Every Single Day

Sri Vijaya Durga Milk Agency is a trusted milk distribution agency located in Gundugolanu, Bhimadolu, Eluru District, Andhra Pradesh. We work with reputed dairy suppliers to deliver fresh, hygienically handled and high-quality milk and dairy products to households, retail outlets, hotels and local businesses.

We believe that fresh milk is not only a product, but a daily essential for every family. That is why our team works early mornings, ensures proper storage and timely delivery and maintains strict quality standards across the supply process.

  • Wholesale supply to retailers & hotels
